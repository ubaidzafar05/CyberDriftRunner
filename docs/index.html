🏃 Cyber Drift Runner

Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 31, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data. We don't sell your personal information. Ads are optional. You're in control.

1. Information We Collect

Cyber Drift Runner collects the following data:

  • Game progress — scores, unlocked items, and settings stored locally on your device via Unity PlayerPrefs.
  • Analytics events — anonymous gameplay events (session start, session length, game over count) to improve game balance. No personal identifiers are attached.
  • Advertising ID — if you consent to personalized ads, your device advertising ID may be shared with our ad partners (Google AdMob).
  • Google Play Games — if you sign in, your display name and profile are provided by Google. We only use this for leaderboards and cloud save.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Display leaderboards and achievements
  • Improve gameplay balance through aggregated, anonymous analytics
  • Show advertisements (personalized only with your consent)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games Services — leaderboards, achievements, cloud save. Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity Analytics — anonymous gameplay telemetry. Unity Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

Cyber Drift Runner displays ads to support free gameplay:

  • Rewarded ads — optional, viewed voluntarily (e.g., revive after death, bonus credits).
  • Interstitial ads — shown at natural breaks (game over screen), limited to once per 3 sessions.
  • Personalized ads — only shown if you explicitly consent via the in-game consent dialog. You can change this in Settings at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

Cyber Drift Runner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud save data (if enabled) is stored in your Google Play Games account.
  • We do not maintain external databases of player information.
  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads — via in-game Settings or the consent dialog.
  • Delete all local data — uninstall the app or clear app data in Android settings.
  • Delete cloud save data — disconnect from Google Play Games in Settings.
  • Reset advertising ID — via your device's Settings > Google > Ads.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance.
